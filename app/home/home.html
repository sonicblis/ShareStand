Our passion is your passion for good food

Welcome to ShareStand, a food sharing site for people as enthusiastic about good food as you are. There are people all over the country that in their zeal for cucumbers (just as an example), planted three cucumber plants. Well, now they are overrun with cucumbers and they want to share the bounty. If you have food to share, click have food above. If you're looking for food from others, click want food and fill your plate.
